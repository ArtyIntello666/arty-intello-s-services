# Pricing Structure

## Hourly Rates
- **Standard Rate:** $100/hour
- **Discounted Rate:** $80/hour for long-term projects (over 100 hours)

## Project-Based Pricing
- **Basic Package:** $1500 for up to 20 hours of work
- **Standard Package:** $3000 for up to 50 hours of work
- **Premium Package:** $5000 for projects exceeding 50 hours, including post-project support

## Tiered Packages
- **Basic Tier:** $200/month for access to up to 5 hours of support
- **Standard Tier:** $350/month for up to 10 hours of support with priority response
- **Premium Tier:** $600/month for unlimited support and advisory services

## Retainer Plans
- **Monthly Retainer:** $2500 for up to 30 hours of work, covering various tasks as needed
- **Quarterly Retainer:** $7000 for up to 100 hours of work
- **Annual Retainer:** $25000 for up to 400 hours of work, offering the best value including special projects and priority assistance.

### Note
- Additional hours beyond the mentioned packages will be billed at the standard hourly rate.
- All prices are subject to change based on market conditions. Please contact us for a detailed quote tailored to your needs.
